Infrastructure Services Market Forecasts

Project Goals

Every other year, IDC publishes Market Forecasts for each component of the Infrastructure Services market.

Project details are protected under an NDA, so the following discussion is generalized and images are open-source representations.

Project Results

I developed forecasts for the Data Center Networking, Private 5G, and Multi-Cloud Networking markets. These forcasts were published and are available for purchase.

For each forecast, I analyzed market data from old reports and existing excel data files. Then, I creating pivot tables to see trends and generate graphs. Finally, I drafted analysis and content for each forecast, which was sent to and edited by the Research VP and President of Network Life-Cycle Services and Infrastructure Services, before publishing.
