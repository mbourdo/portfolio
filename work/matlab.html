Running Social Media

To encourage participation at events, I need to market to students. At Western, most students primarily use Instagram. I created the uwomatlab Instagram and built the account to 136 followers.

On this account, I post educational content to tech students the power of MATLAB. Additionally, I take photos during events to share on the Instagram. This tends to get students more excited about coming to events, because they know they might get featured :).
